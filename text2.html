How to Make Headers of Different Weights


Creating a header for your page is easy!
Inside the tag, to indicate you want to put a header, the tag is: <hx>. Where x could be a number. For an example, <h1>, <h2>, <h3>
The numbers indicate the size of the header with h1 being the largest.
Inside the tags write whatever heading you want!
Note!! You can use <br> to insert breaks into your code
