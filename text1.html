Creating a title for your page is easy! Inside the tag, to indicate you want to put a title, the tag is: . Inside the tags write whatever title you want! EX: <title> How to Give a Page a Title </title> Note!! A tittle after a body is not valid. Even though you can omit the HEAD tag, it is implied when you start a TITLE tag. And the HEAD tag has to be the first tag inside the HTML tag. A title at the end implies a head at the end, which makes your document invalid.
