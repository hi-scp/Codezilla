Different Styles to Texts


Changing the style of text is easy !
Inside the tag, there are various ways to change different parts of a text:
Formatting elements were designed to display special types of text:
<b> - Bold text
<strong> - Important text
<i> - Italic text
<em> - Emphasized text
<mark> - Marked text
<sub> - Subscript text
<sup> - Superscript text
To change an element, make sure you put <p> brackets before your format.
For an example: <p><b>This text is bold.</b></p>
